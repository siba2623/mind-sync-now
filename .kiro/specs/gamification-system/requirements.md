# Gamification & Rewards System - Requirements

## 🎯 Vision
Transform MindSync into an engaging, habit-forming wellness platform where users are motivated to maintain their mental health through fun, rewarding experiences.

## 🎮 Core Features

### 1. Streak Tracking 🔥
**Goal:** Keep users coming back daily

**Features:**
- Daily check-in streak counter
- Streak freeze (1 per week - don't lose streak if you miss a day)
- Streak milestones (7, 30, 100, 365 days)
- Visual streak calendar
- Streak recovery (pay points to restore)
- Longest streak record

**Rewards:**
- 7-day streak: +50 points, Bronze badge
- 30-day streak: +200 points, Silver badge
- 100-day streak: +1000 points, Gold badge
- 365-day streak: +5000 points, Diamond badge

### 2. Achievement Badges 🏆
**Goal:** Recognize accomplishments

**Badge Categories:**
- **Wellness Warrior** - Daily check-ins
- **Meditation Master** - Meditation minutes
- **Mood Tracker** - Mood entries
- **Activity Champion** - Activities completed
- **Peer Helper** - Peer support participation
- **Medication Adherent** - Medication tracking
- **Journal Keeper** - Journal entries
- **Breathing Expert** - Breathing exercises

**Badge Tiers:**
- Bronze (beginner)
- Silver (intermediate)
- Gold (advanced)
- Platinum (expert)
- Diamond (legendary)

### 3. Level System 📊
**Goal:** Show long-term progress

**Levels:**
- Level 1-10: Beginner (0-1000 points)
- Level 11-25: Intermediate (1001-5000 points)
- Level 26-50: Advanced (5001-15000 points)
- Level 51-75: Expert (15001-35000 points)
- Level 76-100: Master (35001+ points)

**Level Benefits:**
- Unlock new features
- Exclusive badges
- Premium themes
- Priority support
- Special challenges

### 4. Daily Challenges 🎯
**Goal:** Drive specific behaviors

**Challenge Types:**
- **Mood Check**: "Log your mood 3 times today" (+20 points)
- **Meditation**: "Meditate for 10 minutes" (+30 points)
- **Activity**: "Complete 2 wellness activities" (+25 points)
- **Journal**: "Write a journal entry" (+15 points)
- **Breathing**: "Do 3 breathing exercises" (+20 points)
- **Social**: "Help someone in peer support" (+40 points)

**Rotation:**
- 3 daily challenges per day
- Refreshes at midnight
- Bonus for completing all 3 (+50 points)

### 5. Weekly Challenges 🗓️
**Goal:** Longer-term engagement

**Examples:**
- "Meditate 5 times this week" (+150 points)
- "Maintain 7-day streak" (+100 points)
- "Complete 10 activities" (+200 points)
- "Help 3 people in peer support" (+250 points)
- "Track mood every day" (+120 points)

### 6. Reward Shop 🛍️
**Goal:** Give points value

**Rewards:**
- **Premium Themes** (500 points) - Unlock special app themes
- **Custom Avatars** (300 points) - Unique profile pictures
- **Streak Freeze** (200 points) - Save your streak
- **Badge Showcase** (100 points) - Display more badges
- **Priority Support** (1000 points) - Jump the queue
- **Ad-Free Month** (800 points) - Remove ads
- **Exclusive Content** (400 points) - Premium meditations
- **Charity Donation** (500 points) - We donate on your behalf

### 7. Leaderboards 🏅
**Goal:** Social competition (opt-in)

**Leaderboard Types:**
- **Weekly Points** - Top earners this week
- **Monthly Streak** - Longest streaks
- **All-Time** - Lifetime points
- **Friends** - Compare with friends only
- **Global** - Everyone (anonymous)

**Privacy:**
- Opt-in only
- Anonymous display names
- Can hide at any time

### 8. Social Sharing 📱
**Goal:** Viral growth

**Shareable:**
- Streak milestones
- Badge unlocks
- Level ups
- Challenge completions
- Leaderboard rankings

**Platforms:**
- Twitter, Facebook, Instagram
- Copy link
- Share to peer support

### Spending Points

- Streak recovery
- Premium features
- Charity donations

## 🔐 Anti-Gaming Measures

### Prevent Abuse
- **Rate limiting** - Max points per action per day
- **Validation** - Server-side verification
- **Cooldowns** - Minimum time between actions
- **Pattern detection** - Flag suspicious behavior
- **Manual review** - For high earners

### Fair Play
- **No point buying** - Earn only through actions
- **No exploits** - Secure implementation
- **Transparent rules** - Clear point system
- **Equal opportunity** - Everyone can succeed
