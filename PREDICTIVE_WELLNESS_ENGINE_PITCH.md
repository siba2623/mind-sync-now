## 🔬 The Science: 5-Stream Intelligence

### 1. **Biometric Synchronicity** (Wearables)
- **What:** Heart rate variability (HRV), sleep quality, activity patterns
- **Prediction:** Nocturnal HRV drops 15% → Depression risk in 5-7 days
- **Evidence:** Frontiers in Psychiatry (2025) - 76% accuracy

### 2. **Digital Phenotyping** (Smartphone Behavior)
- **What:** Typing speed, error rate, app usage, screen time
- **Prediction:** Typing slows 25% → Cognitive impairment/depression
- **Evidence:** BiAffect Study (2024) - r=0.68 correlation

### 3. **Voice Biomarkers** (Passive Audio)
- **What:** Pitch variance, speech rate, pause duration
- **Prediction:** Flat affect detected → Depressive episode imminent
- **Evidence:** Nature Digital Medicine (2023) - 82% accuracy

### 4. **Contextual Intelligence** (Environment)
- **What:** Weather, social isolation, routine disruption
- **Prediction:** <30min sunlight + 3 days alone → High risk
- **Evidence:** Seasonal affective disorder research

### 5. **Self-Report Integration** (Active Check-ins)
- **What:** Emotion trends, PHQ-9 scores, strategy usage
- **Prediction:** Pleasant→Unpleasant shift over 4 days → Intervention needed
- **Evidence:** Yale emotion research + clinical assessments
