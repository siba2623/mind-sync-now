# 🎵 AI-Powered Music Recommendations

## What's New?

We've replaced the complex Spotify API integration with a much better solution: **AI-powered music recommendations using Gemini!**

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 **Smart AI Analysis**
- Analyzes your current mood using Gemini AI
- Provides personalized music therapy insights
- Explains why each song helps with your specific emotional state

### 🎶 **Diverse Music Suggestions**
- 5-8 carefully curated songs per mood
- Mix of genres: pop, classical, indie, ambient, electronic
- Both uplifting and calming options based on your needs

### 🔗 **Multiple Platform Links**
- **YouTube** - Primary platform (red button)
- **Spotify** - For premium users
- **Apple Music** - For iOS users
- Direct search links to find songs instantly

### 💡 **Wellness Integration**
- Mood analysis and explanation
- General wellness advice
- Music therapy insights
- Emotional support through music

## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Mood Detection** - App detects or user selects their current mood
2. **AI Analysis** - Gemini AI analyzes the mood and emotional needs
3. **Music Curation** - AI selects songs that help with that specific mood
4. **Platform Links** - Provides direct links to find songs on major platforms
5. **Wellness Advice** - Offers additional emotional support and guidance

1. Make sure your `VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY` is set in `.env`
2. Select or detect your current mood
3. Click "Get AI Recommendations"
4. Enjoy personalized music therapy!
